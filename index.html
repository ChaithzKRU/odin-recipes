Chicken Dynamite Rice

Chicken dynamite rice is a spicy and flavorful dish made with rice, chicken, and various vegetables such as bell peppers, onions, and carrots. The chicken is marinated in a spicy sauce and then stir-fried with the vegetables before being mixed with the rice. The dish is often garnished with scallions and served with a side of hot sauce or chili oil.

Chicken Fried Rice

Chicken fried rice is a popular Asian dish made with cooked rice, vegetables, eggs, and diced chicken that has been stir-fried in a wok or frying pan with soy sauce and other seasonings.

Con Pollo

Con Pollo is a Spanish phrase that translates to "with chicken" in English. It is often used to describe dishes that include chicken as a primary ingredient, such as arroz con pollo (rice with chicken) or enchiladas con pollo (enchiladas with chicken).
